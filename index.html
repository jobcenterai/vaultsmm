Features

Everything a secure trade needs

Built from the ground up for Discord, Vaults handles every step of the middleman process — so both parties trade with confidence.

Escrow System

Funds and digital goods are held securely in escrow until both parties confirm the deal. No release without mutual confirmation.

Trade Verification

Both buyers and sellers go through a quick identity check before trades begin. Reduces scam risk and builds trust between parties.

Dedicated Trade Channels

Each trade gets its own private channel. Buyer, seller, and the bot — no clutter, no confusion, full audit trail.

Dispute Resolution

If something goes wrong, raise a dispute instantly. Human moderators step in, review evidence, and deliver a fair ruling.

Trade History & Logs

Every completed trade is logged. Build your reputation over time with a clean, verifiable trade record attached to your profile.

Scammer Blacklist

Known scammers are automatically flagged and blocked from initiating new trades. Community-sourced, moderator-verified.

How it works

From open to closed in minutes

Vaults guides both parties through each step automatically. No manual coordination, no back-and-forth confusion.

01

Open a trade

Use go on our ticket channel to start. Vaults creates a private channel and invites both parties.

02

Lock in the deal

Both parties agree on terms inside the channel. The bot records the agreement — no ambiguity, no misremembering.

03

Escrow holds funds

The buyer deposits. Vaults holds everything securely until the seller delivers and the buyer confirms receipt.

04

Release & complete

Both sides confirm. Funds are released, rep is updated, channel is archived. Clean, fast, final.

Security

Why both sides trust Vaults

Vaults isn't just a bot — it's a system built around protecting people. Every feature exists to prevent loss and ensure accountability.

  • Non-custodial escrow flow

    Funds move directly between parties on confirmation — Vaults never has unilateral control over your assets.

  • Both parties must confirm

    No single click releases escrow. The deal only closes when both buyer and seller confirm independently.

  • Immutable trade logs

    Every message and action in a trade channel is logged and cannot be altered — full evidence trail if disputes arise.

  • Mod-reviewed disputes

    Disputes aren't handled by an algorithm. Real moderators review the logs and make fair, informed decisions.
